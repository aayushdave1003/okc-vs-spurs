# ⚡ OKC Thunder

The #1 seed in the West. Built around one of the best young cores in the NBA.

## Key Players

- **Shai Gilgeous-Alexander** — Debatably the best player in this series.
  MVP candidate who can take over any game, though Wemby has something to say about that
- **Jalen Williams** — Major injury concern. Has been dealing with a left hamstring 
  strain for most of the playoffs. He re-aggravated it after just 7 minutes in Game 2 
  of this series, and has missed Games 3, 4, and 5. His Game 7 status is up in the air. 
  OKC is a different team without him — he averaged 14.4 points per game this playoffs
- **Alex Caruso** — Has been on fire, averaging 17 PPG on 57% from three in this series
- **Chet Holmgren** — Stretch big who can shoot and protect the rim

## How They Got Here
Dominant. OKC swept both the first round and the second round without 
dropping a single game. They have looked like the best team in the West 
all playoffs long.

## Why They Win Game 7
OKC has been in big moments before and knows how to close. They have a 
proven track record in elimination games. The Spurs are a young team 
that has never been in a moment this big — that inexperience could cost them.
If Jalen Williams plays, OKC is the clear pick.
