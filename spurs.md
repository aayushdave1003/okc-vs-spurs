# 🌹 San Antonio Spurs

The #2 seed in the West. Built around a generational talent in Victor Wembanyama.

## Key Players

- **Victor Wembanyama** — The most unique player in the NBA. 7'4" with guard skills,
  elite shot blocking, and a silky jumper. Some argue he's already better than SGA.
  Had a 41 point, 24 rebound game earlier this playoffs. The future of basketball 
  is playing right now
- **Stephon Castle** — Second year guard who has been a key piece all playoffs.
  Dished 13 assists in a regular season game this year — elite playmaker
- **Dylan Harper** — 2025 second overall pick. Had a massive 24 point, 11 rebound,
  7 steal game earlier in these playoffs. Rookie playing like a vet

## How They Got Here
Took down Portland in the first round, then knocked out a tough Minnesota team
in the second round 4-2. Forced a Game 7 by winning Game 6 on the road —
a massive statement from a young team.

## Why They Win Game 7
Wemby is an absolute problem that OKC has no real answer for. If Jalen Williams
doesn't play, the Spurs have a massive advantage. This young team has shown 
they can handle pressure — winning on the road in Game 6 proves that.
Wemby drops 35 and the Spurs are headed to the Finals.
