# 🔮 My Game 7 Predictions

One game. Winner goes to the NBA Finals to face the New York Knicks.

## My Pick: OKC Wins

The Thunder are the better team top to bottom. They swept both the first 
and second round and have been the most dominant team in the West all playoffs.
The Spurs are incredible but they have never been in a moment this big.

## Key Storylines

- **Will Jalen Williams play?** — This is the biggest question of the game.
  If he suits up, OKC wins comfortably. If he sits, this is Wemby's game to take over
- **SGA vs Wemby** — Debatably the two best players in the NBA going head to head
  in a winner take all game. Must watch TV
- **Spurs inexperience** — OKC has been in elimination games before. 
  San Antonio has never been in a Game 7 at this stage. Does that matter? We'll find out
- **Alex Caruso** — Been on fire this series. If he stays hot OKC rolls

## Score Prediction
**OKC 112, SAS 104**

SGA drops 38, Wemby puts up a valiant 32 but it's not enough.
The Thunder are headed to the NBA Finals.
